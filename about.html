Project Guidelines
The Website is structured as a database with different spaces I used in my observation mapped across. This project critically examines public spaces in newyork and finds a correlation between green corridorrs and vertical scaping. Often in India, parks are mere decorative elements built by different governments as show-tell items with entry fees, rules, and regulations that hinder people from accessing them with the same ease. The study highlights how discrepancies between the usage of the same space in different cultures and acceptance levels change the visual narrative of the space and how it feels to the user. It tries to enquire how the relationships, rules, and authority with our spaces also alter people's mindsets when they use them. My broader focus will be on keynotes under spatial politics, behavior, and interaction with a visual approach backed by research papers, first-hand observation, and talking with the users.
